Understanding Block vs Inline

Inspired by @frontendcharm

BLOCK are elements that line break to the line after themselves. They span the whole width of available space.
Block elements: div, h1 to h6, p, ul, li, table, blockquote, pre, form

INLINE only take as much space as they require and don't force a linebreak.
Inline elements: span, a, strong, em, img, br, input, abbr, acronym.
