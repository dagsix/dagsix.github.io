The Rules of Hangman
•Player will have 90 seconds to guess the the word on each level or will lose the game.
•Player can guess 10 letters before they are 'hung' and lose the game.
•If a player guesses the word before they are hung, they advance to a new level with a new word.
•There are six levels of gameplay and a continuous random mode.
•Random mode is capped at 100 levels.
•All words contain ONLY lower case letters, hyphens, periods etc. have ALL been removed
•Historical/Famous People may be living or dead
•Movies are all popular, based on top 100 of all time
•Bands are mostly Rock, Metal and Alt-Rock bands (no specific time frame)
•Play fair and eat your vegetables.
•By default the music and random track option are set to OFF.
•Random Mode : Continous mode of answers from any of the three categories.
•Game includes the incredible Sweetalert2 API found here.
•In game music is from freemusicarchive.org and direct from some artist's websites
Artists included: 23:59, Blue Wave Theory, D.A. Dad, fascinating earthbound objects, J Hacha de Zola, Josh Woodward, Ketsa,
KieLoBot, KieLoKaz, King Imagine, Lobo Loco, Los Sundayers, Mabafu, N Dropkick, Nicola with N Dropkick, PEG and The Rejected and Split Phase! Enjoy!
